Phil's Players

A set of video players where you can bring your own HLS or DASH manifest URL for a quick test.

Open Source players

  • DashJS (DASH)
  • HLS.js (HLS)
  • Shaka Player (DASH)
  • VideoJS 7 with VHS (HLS or DASH) or with Mux Data

Commercial players

  • Bitmovin Player (HLS or DASH)
  • Brightcove Player (DASH CENC with Silverlight fallback)
  • Brightcove Player (HLS)
  • Brightcove Player (HLS with Fairplay)
  • JWPlayer (HLS or DASH)
  • THEOplayer (HLS or DASH)
  • Wowza Player (HLS)




Legacy players

These players are old, but remain because some people still have old links to them. Eventually we'll remove these and redirect you to the latest version.

  • VideoJS 5 with contrib-hls (HLS)
  • VideoJS 5 with contrib-dash (DASH) (With Widevine and Chromecast)

This site lets you select a player, enter a HLS or DASH manifest URL, and hit play!
